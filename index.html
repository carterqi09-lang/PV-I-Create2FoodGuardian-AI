Creative ways to turn food scraps into delicious dishes

Vegetable Scrap Broth

Make nutritious broth from onion skins, carrot peels, celery leaves and mushroom stems, perfect for soups and stews.

üç≤ Vegetable Broth Recipe

Required Food Scraps: Onion skins, carrot peels, celery leaves, mushroom stems, garlic skins, etc.

Additional Materials: Water 2L, bay leaf 1 piece, peppercorns 5 grains

Instructions:

  1. Wash all vegetable scraps and place in a large pot
  2. Add water and spices, bring to a boil
  3. Reduce heat and simmer for 1 hour, skimming foam occasionally
  4. Strain out solids, broth can be refrigerated for 3-5 days
  5. Can be used for soups, stews, risottos, etc.

Dishes for Reuse: Vegetable soup, tomato soup, seafood rice, stew

Bread Croutons & Crumbs

Turn stale bread into crispy croutons or breadcrumbs for salads, soups or fried foods.

üçû Homemade Croutons Recipe

Required Food Scraps: Stale bread, bread crusts, baguette, etc.

Additional Materials: Olive oil 2 tbsp, garlic powder 1 tsp, salt a little, spices as needed

Instructions:

  1. Cut bread into 2cm cubes
  2. Place in a bowl, add olive oil, garlic powder, salt and spices, mix well
  3. Spread on a baking sheet, preheat oven to 180¬∞C
  4. Bake for 10-15 minutes until golden and crispy, turn halfway
  5. Cool and store in an airtight container for 1-2 weeks

Dishes for Reuse: Caesar salad, pumpkin soup, roasted vegetables, pasta

Fruit Peel Jam & Syrup

Make natural jam or syrup from apple peels, citrus peels to add flavor to drinks and desserts.

üçä Citrus Peel Syrup Recipe

Required Food Scraps: Citrus peels (orange, lemon, grapefruit, etc.)

Additional Materials: White sugar 200g, water 200ml, lemon juice 1 tbsp

Instructions:

  1. Wash citrus peels, remove white pith (to reduce bitterness)
  2. Cut into thin strips, soak in salt water for 30 minutes to remove bitterness
  3. In a pot, add water, sugar and lemon juice, cook over medium heat until sugar dissolves
  4. Add citrus peel strips, simmer for 15-20 minutes until thickened
  5. Strain and bottle, can be refrigerated for 2-3 weeks

Dishes for Reuse: Cocktails, soda water, pastry glaze, yogurt flavoring

Coffee Grounds Fertilizer & Cleaner

Coffee grounds are rich in nitrogen, phosphorus, and potassium, making them natural fertilizer for acid-loving plants and a gentle abrasive cleaner.

‚òï Coffee Grounds Reuse Methods

Required Food Scraps: Used coffee grounds

Additional Materials: Container, water, soap (optional)

How to use:

  1. Dry coffee grounds to prevent mold
  2. As fertilizer: Sprinkle directly on soil of acid-loving plants (like roses, blueberries, tomatoes)
  3. As cleaner: Mix with a little soap to clean greasy pots and countertops
  4. Deodorizer: Place dry coffee grounds in a small bowl in fridge or shoe cabinet to absorb odors
  5. Body scrub: Mix with coconut oil to make natural body scrub (test skin sensitivity first)

Benefits: Reduce waste, provide plant nutrients, natural cleaning, save money
